Welcome to Japan

Japan is a country unlike any other — a place where ancient temples sit beside neon-lit skyscrapers, where centuries-old customs are observed with quiet pride, and where every meal feels like a celebration. Whether you're drawn by the cuisine, the culture, or the breathtaking scenery, Japan has something that will stay with you long after you leave.

About Japan

Located in East Asia, Japan is an archipelago of over 6,800 islands. Its four main islands — Honshu, Hokkaido, Kyushu, and Shikoku — are home to some of the world's most iconic cities, including Tokyo, Kyoto, and Osaka. Known for its cleanliness, hospitality, and deep respect for tradition, Japan consistently ranks as one of the top travel destinations in the world.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Tokyo
  • Population: ~125 million
  • Language: Japanese
  • Currency: Japanese Yen (¥)
  • Best time to visit: March–May (cherry blossom season) or October–November (fall foliage)
  • Known for: Sushi, anime, Mount Fuji, cherry blossoms, bullet trains, and warm hospitality
